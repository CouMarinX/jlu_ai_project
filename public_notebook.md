## active function

$$
\begin{align}
\text{sigmoid(x)} &= \frac{1}{1+e^{-x}} \\
\text{Relu}(x) &= 
\begin{cases} 
x, & \text{if } x \geq 0 \\ 
0, & \text{if } x < 0 
\end{cases} \\
\tanh(x) &= \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}}
\end{align}
$$
